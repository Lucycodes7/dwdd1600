Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration of Independence and the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned polymath, inventor, and diplomat who was one of the most respected American figures of his time.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress and one of the first to sign the Declaration, famously known for his bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A key leader in the movement for American independence and a prominent figure in events like the Boston Tea Party.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution for independence, which led to the Declaration of Independence.

These men, along with many others, played vital roles in America's fight for independence from Britain.
